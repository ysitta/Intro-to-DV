Data Visualization

How you will learn to tell stories with data?

  1. Understand the context
  2. Who is your audience? What do you need them to know or do?
  3. Choose an appropriate visual display
  4. Tell a story and making point
  5. Most data visualization is done for the purpose of communication

The Goal of Visualization

  • Exploratory
    to uncover data
    to analyze data

  • Explanatory
    to communicate data
    to present data
